Animals

Animals are living beings that move, eat, and respond to their environment. They come in many forms, from tiny insects to large mammals.

LION

Bears are large mammals found in forests and
mountains around the world. They are omnivores,
eating both plants and animals.

Lions are powerful big cats that live in groups
called prides. They are known as the "king of
the jungle" for their strength and dominance.

Sharks are ancient fish with sharp teeth and
excellent senses for hunting. They play an
important role in keeping ocean ecosystems balanced.
