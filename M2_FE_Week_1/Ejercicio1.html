About Social Media

A social network is an online platform that allows people to create
profiles,connect with others, share content, and communicate through
posts,messages, or media.

Conclusion

Social networks have transformed the way people communicate, share
information, and build communities worldwide. They influence culture,
business, and politics by amplifying voices and spreading ideas instantly.
